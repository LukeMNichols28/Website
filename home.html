The Larrosa Research Group is a dynamic and innovative team specialising in organic chemistry and catalysis, with a particular focus on developing new synthetic methodologies. Led by Professor Igor Larrosa, the group is renowned for its pioneering work in C-H activation, a process that allows for the direct functionalization of carbon-hydrogen bonds. We aim to create a collaborative environment which fosters creativity and scientific excellence
