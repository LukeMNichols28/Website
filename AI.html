Artificial Intelligence

A thorough understanding of a reaction's mechanism can act as a roadmap for rationally improving catalysts. However, the route to mechanistic understanding can often be daunting and require significant time and effort. Artificial intelligence (AI) allows for a significant reduction in this barrier, providing a more streamlined aproach to mechanistic elucidation.
